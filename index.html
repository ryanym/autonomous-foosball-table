Idea - Man vs Machine

Janken (rock-paper-scissors) robot with 100% winning from University of Tokyo


Project Goals

Fairness - Follow foosball rules

Playable - Response less than 200 ms

Intelligent - Block at least 70% of the shots from average players

                - Score 50% of the shots goal is unopposed

Affordable - Build cost less than 600 CAD

Safety - Does not harm human players physicall
